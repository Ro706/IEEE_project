About The Institute

Symbiosis Institute of Technology (SIT) Nagpur, established in 2021 under the visionary leadership of Dr. S.B. Mujumdar, follows the principle of 'Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam' (World as One Family). As part of Symbiosis International University, it offers specialized B.Tech programs in Computer Science and Engineering with honors in various fields. Situated in Nagpur, renowned for its livability and education prospects, SIT focuses on delivering top-notch technical education through a curriculum emphasizing both fundamentals and cutting-edge advancements. With a state-of-the-art infrastructure and diverse amenities, SIT fosters holistic development through academic, cultural, and industry engagement activities, aiming to shape global engineers. Admission is through JEE or state entrance exams, reflecting SIT's commitment to excellence in engineering education. Read more here
